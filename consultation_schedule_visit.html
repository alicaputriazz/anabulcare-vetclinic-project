Frequently Asked Questions

AnabulCare is a service platform dedicated to providing veterinary care and support for pets. It offers consultations, advice, and resources to ensure the health and well-being of animals, with a focus on convenience and accessibility for pet owners.

Booking a consultation with AnabulCare can typically be done through their website or app, where you can choose between different services and schedule an appointment with a professional, either online or in person, depending on your needs.

The primary difference between online and offline consultations lies in the mode of interaction. Online consultations are conducted via video calls or chats, allowing pet owners to consult with veterinarians remotely, while offline consultations are in-person appointments at a physical location for hands-on examination and treatment.

Payments for consultations and services can usually be made online via secure payment methods, such as credit cards, bank transfers, or payment apps, at the time of booking. Payment details will be provided during the booking process.

AnabulCare may have physical locations where you can visit for in-person consultations or purchase pet care products. You can find specific store details, including address and contact information, on their website or app.

When purchasing pet products, shipping can vary depending on the retailer or online store you're buying from. Most stores offer standard shipping, express shipping, or sometimes free shipping promotions, with delivery times ranging from a few days to over a week, depending on the shipping option and location. Some retailers may also offer international shipping if you're purchasing from abroad. It’s important to check the store’s shipping policy for details on fees, delivery times, and whether they ship to your location.

If you need to request a refund for a pet product, the process typically involves contacting the retailer’s customer service team. Most stores offer refunds for products that are faulty or damaged, and many will allow returns within a set period after purchase (e.g., 30 days). Refund policies vary, so it’s crucial to review the store’s return and refund policy before making a purchase. Some stores may require you to return the product in its original packaging or provide proof of purchase. Once the return is approved, the refund will usually be processed to your original payment method.
These general guidelines can help you navigate the shipping and refund processes when buying pet products online or in stores. Always be sure to check the specific terms and conditions of the retailer you're buying from for more detailed information.
